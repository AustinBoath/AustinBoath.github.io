Excavating the Nether on 8b8t

Members of the 8b8t community, we are excavating the nether out 1k x 1k, which will be 500 blocks in each direction


How do I contribute

If you wish to help us out, enter the nether on 8b8t & type the following commands out in the correct order into your Minecraft chat (You will need to have a client with Baritone, like Impact or Kami Blue)


#sel 1 500 120 500
# sel 2 -500 122 -500
#sel cleararea

You should now be excavating netherrack using baritone. Go AFK overnight to support the project, but be aware that baritone may stop due to lava. Try turning on AutoEat as well and have gaps in your inventory as well as totems.


Thanks for helping the 8b8t excavating project
